A Symphony of Distances: Our Story

A journey of hearts coming together

Vishal & Arpita

They say economists analyze patterns, but nothing could have predicted the complex, beautiful trajectory that began in August 2019. Within the revered walls of the Delhi School of Economics, two worlds quietly aligned. He was the quintessential Delhi boy, occupying the front of the lecture hall, sharp and focused. She was the girl from UP, seeking knowledge, seated quietly at the back. Across that crowded classroom, glances were exchanged—silent precursors to a future neither could yet fathom.

It was only after a few weeks of shared lectures and stolen glances that they finally exchanged their first words. Friendship blossomed instantly, fueled by shared intellect and laughter. Yet, for a year and a half, a quiet hesitation lingered. They became inseparable friends, their bond growing deeper, their conversations longer, all while the unspoken truth of their love grew quietly beneath the surface.

December 2020: The Turning Point

The dam broke on December 2nd, 2020. He finally summoned the courage to speak his heart. But she hesitated; her eyes were set on the distant horizon—a PhD in the US. She said no, but the refusal tasted of regret. The subsequent forty-eight hours were a silent internal battle against feelings too potent to ignore.

On December 4th, 2020, she called him. The requested venue was sacred: Bangla Sahib Gurudwara. They walked its serene perimeter as friends, but on the car ride home, she could contain her heart no longer. Amidst the movement of Delhi traffic, she turned to him and finally said 'Yes'.

Love Across Miles

That year in Delhi was a sun-drenched season of shared dreams and indelible memories. But love, they soon learned, would be tested by geography. In December 2021, she moved to Bangalore for work, marking the start of their long-distance journey. They adjusted to the miles, unaware that the distance was about to stretch across an ocean.

For years, their romance was measured in time zones, late-night video calls, and the unwavering conviction that they were meant for each other. Time, rather than fraying their connection, only revealed its unbreakable strength. They knew, regardless of where their paths led, they had to walk them together.

A Promise Kept

On December 2nd, 2025—exactly five years after his first confession—they got engaged. And on December 4th, 2026, precisely six years to the day she said 'Yes', Vishal and Arpita will marry.

For now, their home remains the quiet spaces between heartbeats, spanning the vastness between Delhi and Pittsburgh. They will endure the distance, whether measured in months or years, fueled by the certainty that no matter how far they are, they are always, invariably, coming home to one another.
