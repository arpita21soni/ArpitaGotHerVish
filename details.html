Event Schedule

🌸 Haldi

December 3rd, 2026
11:00 AM
Atrio by Devam

Haldi is a pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom to make them glow on their wedding day. Join us for this vibrant and colorful celebration filled with music, laughter, and blessings.

💃 Sangeet & Sagan

December 3rd, 2026
7:00 PM
Atrio by Devam

Sangeet is an evening of music and dance where family members perform. Sagan includes the exchange of gifts and sweets. A night full of entertainment, joy, and anticipation for the big day!

💍 Varmala & Phere

December 4th, 2026
1:00 PM
Atrio by Devam

The main wedding ceremony. Varmala is the exchange of flower garlands, and Phere are the sacred rounds around the fire. A solemn and beautiful moment where two souls unite with blessings from family and friends.

Atrio by Devam

Celebrate with us as we officially begin our married life! Enjoy dinner, dancing, and create lasting memories with friends and family as we step into this new chapter together.

What to Bring & Wear

Dress Code: Traditional Indian attire is encouraged. Men can wear sherwanis, kurtas, or formal suits. Women can wear sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, or ethnic wear. Festive and bright colors are welcome!

What to Consider:

  • Comfortable shoes (as there will be standing and walking)
  • Accessories and jewelry to complete your look
  • A light shawl or jacket for air-conditioned venues
  • Camera to capture memorable moments
