Frequently Asked Questions

When is the wedding and what time should I arrive? ▼
The wedding will take place on December 4th, 2026. Haldi and Sangeet are on December 3rd. We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before the scheduled event time. Exact timings for each event are:
  • Haldi: 11:00 AM on December 3rd
  • Sangeet & Sagan: 7:00 PM on December 3rd
  • Varmala & Phere: 1:00 PM on December 4th
  • Reception: 7:00 PM on December 4th
What should I wear? Are there specific dress codes? ▼
Dress Code: Traditional Indian attire is encouraged and appreciated!
  • Men: Sherwanis, kurtas, churidars, or formal suits
  • Women: Sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, or ethnic wear
  • Colors: Bright, festive colors are welcome. Avoid white and black if possible
Please wear comfortable shoes as you'll be standing and walking during events.
Do I need to RSVP? What's the deadline? ▼
Yes! We need an accurate headcount for planning purposes. Please RSVP by November 20th, 2026 on our RSVP page. If you haven't received an invitation but would like to attend, please contact us.
Can I bring someone as my plus one? ▼
Whether you can bring a plus one depends on your invitation. Check your invitation card for details. If you have questions about bringing a guest, please contact us and we'll be happy to help.
What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies? ▼
Please mention your dietary requirements during the RSVP process. There's a section dedicated to dietary preferences and restrictions. If you need to add or modify dietary information after RSVPing, please contact us with your details.
Is there parking available at the venue? ▼
Yes, parking is available at Atrio by Devam. For specific details about parking arrangements or if you have special parking requirements, please contact us.
Are children welcome? Are there kids' activities? ▼
Yes, children are welcome! We have celebrations that the whole family can enjoy. Please let us know about children attending during RSVP. Activities and arrangements for kids will be made accordingly. For specific details, please contact us.
Will there be accommodation arrangements? ▼
Please contact us for details about accommodation. We can help guests who are coming from out of town.
Can I bring my camera or phone to take pictures? ▼
Of course! We'd love for you to capture and share memories. Please feel free to take photos and videos throughout the events. Professional photographers will also be there, so you can share your perspective!
When will wedding photos be available? ▼
Professional photos and videos from the wedding will be available after the events. We'll share a link to a cloud storage or gallery where you can view and download photos. Check back on our gallery page after December 4th for the full collection!
I have accessibility needs. How can I ensure accommodations? ▼
Your comfort is important to us. Please mention any accessibility requirements during RSVP or contact us directly to discuss your specific needs. We'll do our best to arrange appropriate accommodations.
